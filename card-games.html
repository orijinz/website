Our Card Games

Orijinz card games come in two flavors — both dangerously addictive.

Words & Phrases

The original classic, with two editions, each packed with 150 unique cards. One player reads the origin story; everyone else battles to guess the matching word or phrase. Cue the “Wait… I know this!” face.

Quotes

Orijinz Quotes flips the script. Instead of origins, you get a cascade of quotes — funny, profound, surprising — and players race to name the author. If the first quote stumps everyone, read the next one and watch the lightbulbs go off.

Both games take about five minutes to learn. You don’t need to be a word nerd, a trivia shark, or a history buff — just curious and ready to laugh. One customer even called Orijinz “the best thinking game for adults in a long time.” Teens love it too, and it earned a Parent’s Choice Approved Award.

Play on road trips, around the dinner table, at the kitchen counter, during game night, or stash a couple in the office lunchroom. Join the tens of thousands of players around the world who’ve discovered that Orijinz is a cut above — the true pick of the litter.
