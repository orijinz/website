Q&A

What is Orijinz Daily?

Orijinz Daily is a daily puzzle. You get one puzzle for each game you are signed up for every day. You read a clue, such as an origin, and guess the answer, such as the word or phrase that has that origin. A timer starts when you begin.

How many Orijinz Daily games are there?

There are currently six games:

  • Words & Phrases: Read the origin, guess the matching word or phrase.
  • 70s Songs: Read the song title. Guess the matching artist.
  • Covers: Listen to the cover. Guess the original artist.
  • Movies: Read a movie synopsis. Guess the movie.
  • Slogans: Read the slogan. Guess the source.
  • Quotes: Read the quote. Guess the source.

Can you get hints to help you answer the puzzle?

Yes, different games have different types of hints. All games let you tap 🔡 Reveal a Letter and a random letter from the answer is revealed. You can choose 🔡 Reveal a Letter as many times as you want, even to show the full answer. Some games have other buttons to give you a hint. In Words & Phrases, some clues let you tap 📖 Definition. In 70s Songs, you can tap 🎧 Listen for up to a 30 second audio clip. In Movies, you can tap ▶ Watch Trailer for up to a 30 second trailer. In Quotes, sometimes there is a general 💡 Hint.

How is my score calculated?

For all games, the lower the score the better. Your score starts with your time. All games add 15 seconds per letter revealed and 5 seconds per wrong letter guessed. Additional time is added based on other hints you use. Check Help for each game for specifics. For example, your score for Words & Phrases = your time + 30 seconds for using the definition + 15 seconds per letter revealed + 5 seconds per wrong letter guessed.

Where do I see everyone’s scores?

After you play, tap Results to view your results and the results of other players on the Orijinz leaderboard. There are three tabs — Score, Games Played, and Streak (games played in a row). If you click the arrows next to “Today’s Results” you can see the outcomes for each of the past three days.

Can I change my name or remove my name from the Leaderboard?

Yes. To change your name, go to the menu on the upper left and select “Account Settings.” Tap the “Display name” input box, change your name and then tap the “Update Info” button. To remove your name from the leaderboard, click the menu, choose “Settings” and toggle off “Show me on the leaderboard.”

Can I share my results with others?

A key part of the fun of Orijinz Daily is sharing your results with others, convincing them to play, comparing your results, and discussing the clues. Next to Results is Share! — tap it to share your results.

Can I view my statistics over time?

Yes, you can view and share your stats. Click on the menu on the upper left of the screen and choose “Statistics.” You can see your stats for the game you are playing on the default “This Game” tab. Select the “Today” tab to see the results from the games you have played today or the “All Games” tab to see your stats across all games you have subscribed to.

Is there a way to go back and play an Orijinz Daily puzzle from an earlier day?

Unfortunately, not.
