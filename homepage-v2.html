The fun and fascinating origins games

For over a decade, Orijinz has been the little guessing game that could — surprising, delighting, and occasionally stumping players around the world. It all started with our card games — read an origin, guess the matching word or phrase or read a quote and guess the voice behind it. After that, Orijinz Daily arrived with one fresh puzzle every morning that you can play on your computer, phone or laptop.

Now we’re leveling up.

Introducing the new Orijinz Daily lineup — a revamped Words & Phrases plus five brand‑new daily games: 70s Songs, Covers, Movies, Slogans, and Quotes. Six games. Six daily brain-tingles. Endless “wow, that was fascinating,” “oooh, that was fun,” “that was so cool,” and “I didn’t know that” moments.

Sign up for any game and enjoy a two‑week free trial. After that, each game is just $1.99/month, or grab the Orijinz Daily Game Pass for an introductory $5.99/month and play all the games, every day.

Some puzzles are easy. Some will make you pace around the room. All of them are fun, clever, interesting, addictive and a blast to share and compete with friends and family. Want to learn more? Just go to the menu on the upper left and choose Orijinz Daily Q&A. You can download the Orijinz Daily App on that menu too.

Give Orijinz Daily a spin — your brain will thank you.
